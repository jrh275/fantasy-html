Top Five Epic Fantasy Series

The genre of fantasy is wide ranging. Epic fantasy is considered by most to multi-volume stories that attempt to resolve existential crises for their world's inhabitants.
Magic systems are not required but are usually present. Let's break down my top five favorites.

  1. The Lord of the Rings by J.R.R. Tolkein
  2. The Wheel of Time by Robert Jordan
  3. The Kingkiller Chronicles by Patrick Rothfuss
  4. A Song of Ice and Fire by George R.R. Martin
  5. Memory, Sorrow and Thorn by Tad Williams
